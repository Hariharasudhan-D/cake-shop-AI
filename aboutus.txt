# About Us

Welcome to Cake Shop AI, where every cake is crafted with love, care, and the warmth of home. Our journey began with a simple dream: to create delicious and healthy homemade cakes that bring happiness to every celebration.

For the past five years, we have been dedicated to baking a wide variety of cakes using only high-quality homemade ingredients. We strongly believe that food should be both tasty and healthy. That is why our cakes are prepared without harmful chemicals, artificial colors, preservatives, or unnecessary additives. Every cake is made in a clean and hygienic environment, ensuring freshness and quality in every bite.

The heart behind our bakery is Geeta, a passionate homemaker with a vision to become independent and build something meaningful. With creativity and dedication, she prepares each cake beautifully, making every order special for customers. Her commitment to taste and quality has earned the trust and love of many families.

We offer a wide range of cakes to suit different preferences, including brown sugar cakes, wheat cakes, and many other varieties. Whether it is a birthday, anniversary, family gathering, or any special occasion, we have the perfect cake for you.

To make celebrations even more convenient, home delivery services are also available. At Cake Shop AI, we are not just baking cakes—we are creating sweet memories with every order. Thank you for supporting homemade goodness and being a part of our journey.
